Exploring Concepts

For this project, I developed the identity for Modis, a fashion academy that positions itself as modern, editorial, and avant-garde. The concept was inspired by the idea of ‘an eye for fashion.’ This is expressed through the logotype, where the O in Modis becomes the eye itself, symbolizing vision and perspective.

The logo goes further, combining two O’s with a small head to form the silhouette of a figure in a flowing dress.

Process

The project began with sketches, exploring different ways to capture the essence of vision and fashion through shapes and symbols. From these hand-drawn ideas, I refined the concept digitally, experimenting with proportions, compositions, and variations of the “eye” and flowing dress forms.

I refined the logo and logotype in Adobe Illustrator, focusing on clean lines and adaptability. With Photoshop, I created editorial-style mockups to test textures and photographic contexts, ensuring a modern, dynamic feel.

Visual Impact & Execution

Across applications, the system maintains a modern editorial tone while adapting seamlessly to different formats—print, digital, and motion. Animated studies further enhance its impact, ensuring that Modis feels alive, relevant, and visually striking in both static and moving contexts.

Project Complete

This project transformed the idea of “an eye for fashion” into a bold, versatile identity. Through sketches, digital design, and motion, Modis came to life as a modern, editorial, and avant-garde brand.
