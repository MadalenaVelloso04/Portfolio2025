Brand Concept

Olura is a women’s supplement brand focused on hormonal balance and embracing every change. The identity combines a soft, feminine aesthetic with a playful and natural tone, expressed through a warm pink palette and graceful motifs.

Each supplement is distinguished by a unique natural illustration, reflecting its purpose—whether for skin, hair, stress, or other needs—creating a system that feels both cohesive and diverse. The packaging comes in two formats: a larger container for powders and a cardboard box with glass pill jars, balancing practicality with elegance.

Process

The brand was developed using a mix of mockups and short videos to showcase the key visuals in context. Soft, atmospheric presentations highlight the feminine and natural aesthetic, while dynamic clips bring movement and life to the identity, emphasizing Olura’s playful, graceful, and approachable personality.

Soft overlays, mixed media and blured photography are used all thoughout as the main technique to achieve this atmosphere.

Visual Impact & Execution

Olura's packaging combines graceful natural motifs with a soft pink palette, creating a feminine and uplifting presence. The two packaging formats—powder containers and cardboard boxes with glass jars—maintain elegance and functionality, while mockups and short videos bring the brand to life in a playful, atmospheric way.

Project Complete

Olura blends care, beauty, and playfulness into a cohesive brand. Through thoughtful packaging, natural motifs, and dynamic visuals, the identity supports women in every stage of change with elegance and warmth.
