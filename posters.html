Exploring Photoshop

This collection is a static gallery showcasing my progress and experimentation, primarily in Photoshop. Posters are one of my favorite forms of communication—simple, impactful, and effective—which is why they hold such importance for me as both art and design. For this reason, I want to highlight them in my portfolio.

Layout, form, and color, all matters and all is thought of throughout each work.

Process

The process begins with exploration and experimentation in Photoshop, testing layouts, color, and typography. Each poster is developed with a focus on clarity and impact, refining ideas through iterations until the visual communicates effectively and resonates as both art and design.

Each iteration focuses on experimentation with color, layout, and typography, allowing the concept to evolve naturally while keeping the poster’s message clear and strong.
